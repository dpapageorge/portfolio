02How I actually work

Four opinions I’ll bring to your team.

Not a tasteful list of values, the actual stances I’ll argue for in your roadmap meeting.

  1. 01

    Delete before you design

    The most valuable thing I shipped at Switchfly was often the step I removed. I hunt for what can disappear before I polish what’s left. It’s faster, cheaper, and kinder to the person using it.

  2. 02

    If you can’t draw it, you don’t get it

    Fifteen years in, I still sketch every system by hand before I open Figma. A pen finds the gaps in your thinking that pixels are happy to paper over.

  3. 03

    A design system is a settled argument

    On a platform that ships continuously, the system is the only thing keeping a thousand small decisions from drifting apart. I build the agreement, not just the components.

  4. 04

    Research is how you stop being wrong in public

    Wireframes and opinions get a team moving; testing keeps it honest. I’d rather be corrected by five users in a room than by the whole market after launch.
